Public Research Claim

An AI-Native Learning Organization is an organizational model in which humans and AI systems jointly produce work, observe outcomes, extract learning, and convert that learning into reusable organizational capability.

This is a research program, not a claim of completed validation.

Disclosure Boundary

This public dashboard excludes raw logs, private decisions, unpublished sensitive evidence, customer data, credentials, and detailed internal blockers.

When in doubt, information remains private.

Public Theory Map

TH-01

Organizational Learning Loop

Work creates observations; review extracts learning; updated artifacts improve later work.

TH-02

Organizational Memory Hierarchy

Context should be retrieved by level and relevance rather than loaded as a flat history.

TH-03

Doctrine-Mediated Governance

Doctrine defines judgment principles and review boundaries between humans and AI agents.
